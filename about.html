ABOUT THE COMPANY

At AM Roofing, we only employ skilled tradespeople who are certified to do their job. All of our crew members are full-time, trained and certified employees. We maintain a meticulous cleanup policy on every job site. We have insurance and WSIB Coverage. We also offer roof inspections and a full line of repair services. When you have a new roof installed it is a great opportunity to review the insulation in your home. Does your home meet today's building code for insulation? If not, this would be the time to install Owens Corning Insulation. Save money, increase your home energy rating, improve the comfort inside your home and help the environment. Emergency and temporary repair services are available around the clock.

3 Generations of AM Roofing

In 1956 Harold Pearce established AM Roofing. He brought his knowledge and hard work ethic from a small town called Coatbridge, Scotland, to Canada in the early 1950's. He built the business on integrity, pride of workmanship, quality products and his desire to be known as the very best roofing company in the area. Harold's son joined AM Roofing in 1973 and together they continued to earn the reputation as a highly trusted and professional business. To this day, AM Roofing is recognized as the leader in their field and a role model in the roofing industry.

AM Roofing Family Mission & Values

At AM Roofing we still take pride in offering exceptional workmanship, meticulous ground care and clean-up, excellent manufacturer warranties and our most important offering: a 10-year fully transferable written workmanship warranty and complete satisfaction. We are proud to stamp the AM Roofing name on every roof we install. We stand behind our work and our workers with Workman's Compensation and General Liability Insurance. The AM Roofing Family are professionals that keep all of their safety training up to date.
