Commercial Roofing

As a building owner, choosing the right commercial roofing solution can be one of the most important decisions you can make. Choose the right commercial roofing system and protect your investment for decades. Choose the wrong system and be prepared to spend untold dollars on constant repairs and possibly a complete replacement. At AM Roofing Solutions, we have a dedicated team of professional roofing technicians that have over 50 years of industry experience and we can advise you on the best course of action for a commercial roofing repair or replacement. The roof atop your building is a four-stage system that operates dependently on each system to provide protection from the elements; surface treatment, ventilation, insulation and a drainage system are the components that make up the system. When one fails, it causes stress on the other components causing degradation to your commercial roofing system

Many older building have a combination of tar, paper and gravel that is applied in layers to seal the roof tightly to protect it against the elements with an average lifespan of 10 years.

The advent of new roofing technology has provided commercial roofing with a membrane system that offers clear advantages to the commercial roofing industry

  • Installation is quicker with the membrane system and it is
    environmentally-friendly
  • The membrane extends the lifespan of the commercial roofing solution
  • The rubber membrane provides greater durability for the roofing system to stand up to the elements
  • Reduced post-installations problems arise with a membrane roofing system
  • AM Roofing Solutions uses two different varieties of membrane systems to provide commercial roofing system solutions. The most common is EPDM; this solution encapsulates a rubber membrane with gravel ballast applied to the top of it.

The other commercial roofing solution that is gaining in popularity is TPO - a relatively new technological advancement in roofing solutions and it uses heat to bind the membrane to the exterior to the building. TPO was designed for larger commercial roofing systems, is more cost effective and is white in design to reflect heat away from the roof and can assist in lowering building cooling costs. The certified technicians at AM Roofing Solutions are trained and experienced in installation of this type of commercial roofing solution and carry special insurance to work with this material installation system.
